# Wireless Products Research (Manufacturers + Product Pages)

## Overview
This repository documents a short research task on common wireless/IoT products and modules.  
For each product type, I provide a manufacturer, a link to the official manufacturer/product page, and a brief description of its purpose and notable features.

---

## 1) LoRaWAN Gateway
**Manufacturer:** Kerlink  
**Link:** https://www.kerlink.com/product/wirnet-ifemtocell/  
**Description:** A LoRaWAN gateway bridges LoRaWAN end devices (sensors/actuators) to IP networks, forwarding packets to a LoRaWAN network server. Kerlink’s Wirnet iFemtoCell is built for deep indoor coverage and is often used to densify networks in challenging RF environments.

---

## 2) Zigbee Module
**Manufacturer:** Silicon Labs  
**Link:** https://www.silabs.com/wireless/zigbee/efr32mg21-series-2-modules/device.mgm210pa32jia  
**Description:** A Zigbee module provides low-power mesh connectivity for devices such as smart lighting, sensors, and home automation products. Silicon Labs’ MGM210P module is based on the EFR32MG21 family and supports robust, scalable mesh networking for IoT deployments.

---

## 3) Wi-Fi 6 Chipset
**Manufacturer:** Qualcomm  
**Link:** https://www.qualcomm.com/wi-fi/products/fastconnect/fastconnect-6700  
**Description:** A Wi-Fi 6 chipset enables high-throughput WLAN connectivity and is commonly used in phones, laptops, and embedded devices. Qualcomm FastConnect 6700 targets high performance and efficiency, supporting modern Wi-Fi 6 features for improved capacity in busy networks.

---

## 4) NB-IoT Module
**Manufacturer:** Quectel  
**Link:** https://www.quectel.com/product/lpwa-bg95-cat-m1-cat-nb2-egprs-series/  
**Description:** An NB-IoT module provides low-power cellular connectivity optimized for small, infrequent data transmissions from sensors and trackers. Quectel BG95 is a multi-mode LPWA module (NB-IoT/LTE-M with fallback options in the series) designed for long battery life and wide-area coverage.

---

## 5) Sigfox Device (Module)
**Manufacturer:** WiSOL (Sigfox Partner Network listing)  
**Link:** https://partners.sigfox.com/products/sfm10r1  
**Description:** A Sigfox device/module supports ultra-narrowband IoT messaging where power efficiency and coverage matter more than data rate. The WiSOL SFM10R1 integrates RF functionality and an MCU to help manufacturers build compact Sigfox-enabled products.

---

## 6) Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Beacon
**Manufacturer:** Kontakt.io  
**Link:** https://store.kontakt.io/product/puck-beacon/  
**Description:** A BLE beacon broadcasts advertisement packets that nearby phones or gateways can detect for proximity, location-based services, or asset tracking. Kontakt.io’s Puck Beacon is designed for practical deployments and supports BLE 5.0 with configurable beacon parameters.

---

## 7) Ultra-Wideband (UWB) Transceiver
**Manufacturer:** Qorvo  
**Link:** https://www.qorvo.com/products/p/DW3110  
**Description:** A UWB transceiver enables secure, highly accurate ranging and positioning for applications such as access control, tracking, and spatial awareness. Qorvo DW3110 supports IEEE 802.15.4z and integrates features aimed at precise distance measurement and enhanced security.

---
